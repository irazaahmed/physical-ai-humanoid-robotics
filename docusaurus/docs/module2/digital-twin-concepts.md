# Digital Twin Concepts and Architecture

## Learning Objective
Understand the theory and applications of digital twins in robotics with focus on simulation environments.

## Key Concepts
- Digital twin architecture and components
- Real-time synchronization between physical and virtual systems
- Sensor fusion in digital twin systems
- Predictive modeling and closed-loop systems
- Validation methodologies for digital twins

## Lab/Practical
Create a simple digital twin model of a basic robot in simulation environment.
